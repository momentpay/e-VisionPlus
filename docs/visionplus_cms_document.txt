
Excellent choice. If you want to understand VisionPLUS like an issuer processor, acquiring switch architect, or card management SME, CMS (Credit Management System) is the first module to master because almost every other module depends on it.
CMS is often called the "Heart of VisionPLUS" because it stores customer, account, card, balance, limit, billing, payment, and account history information. Other modules such as FAS, ASM, CTA, ITS, and TRAMS retrieve or update data in CMS. (Learn Payment Card)

If FAS authorizes transactions and CMS stores accounts, then the Billing & Statement Processing Engine is what actually converts card usage into receivables, interest income, fees, and collection activities.
For issuers, this is the most critical business process because:
Transactions ? Billing ? Statement ? Payment ? Interest ? Revenue

1. What is Billing in CMS?
Billing is the periodic process that:
1. Identifies all transactions for an account
2. Calculates balances
3. Calculates interest
4. Calculates fees
5. Determines minimum payment
6. Generates statement
7. Generates due date
8. Updates delinquency tracking

Simple Example
Customer activity during month:
01-Jan Purchase         1,000
10-Jan Purchase           500
15-Jan Cash Advance       200
20-Jan Annual Fee         100

Total Outstanding       1,800
Billing runs:
Statement Balance = 1,800
Minimum Due = 90
Due Date = 25-Feb

2. Billing Cycle Concept

Therefore:
